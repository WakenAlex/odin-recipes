Zacusca

Description

Zacusca is a Romanian vegetable spread made from roasted eggplants, peppers, onions, and tomatoes. It's seasoned with garlic, salt, and pepper, and sometimes includes chili peppers for heat. The ingredients are sautéed, simmered until thickened, then cooled and stored in jars. Zacusca is versatile, served as a spread on bread or crackers, or used as a condiment for various dishes.

Ingredients

  • Eggplants
  • Red bell peppers
  • Yellow or green bel peppers
  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Onions, finely chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: hot chili peppers for extra heat

Steps

  1. Roast eggplants, peppers, tomatoes
  2. Peel and chop roasted veggies
  3. Sauté onions and garlic
  4. Combine all chopped veggies
  5. Season with salt and pepper
  6. Optional: add chili peppers for heat
  7. Simmer mixture until thickened
  8. Cool completely
  9. Transfer to sterilized jars
  10. Seal tightly
  11. Store in the refrigerator
  12. Serve on bread or crackers. Enjoy!
